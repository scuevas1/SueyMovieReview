Everything Everywhere All At Once

My Review

This has to be one of the best movies I have seen. The film is a wild, imaginative, and hilarious ride that explores the multiverse in a way that has never been done before. It is also a deeply moving and personal film that explores family, identity, and self-acceptance themes. The cast in this movie is extremely strong. Each actor/actress brings a different and powerful vibe. I definitely recommend this movie.

Synopsis

When an interdimensional rupture unravels reality, an unlikely hero must channel her newfound powers to fight bizarre and bewildering dangers from the multiverse as the fate of the world hangs in the balance.
